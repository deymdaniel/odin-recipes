Pork Adobo

Classic Pork Adobo Recipe

This Pork Adobo recipes combines sweet, salty, and acidic flavor that'll keep you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Peppercorn
  • Bayleaf
  • Datu Puti Soy Sauce
  • Datu Puti Vinegar
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Add oil to a hot pan
  2. Add the garlic and onions and cook until aromatic
  3. Add the Pork Belly and cook until browned and fat starts to render
  4. Add Soy Sauce and enough water to cover the pork
  5. Add the Bayleaves and Peppercorn for more aroma
  6. Simmer the pork on low heat until tender
  7. Once the sauce has reduced and the pork is tender, add the Vinegar but dont mix it to remove the raw taste of the Vinegar
  8. Lastly, add the Brown Sugar to thicken the sauce
  9. Plate and enjoy the sweet, salty, and acidic sauce paired with the soft and tender pork belly
