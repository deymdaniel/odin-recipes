Egg Fried Rice

Quick and Easy Egg Fried Rice

This Egg Fried Rice recipe is quick, easy, and delicous that'll elivate any dish you pair it with

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Day-old Rice
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • MSG
  • White Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Beat 2-3 eggs
  2. Add oil to a really hot pan
  3. Add beaten eggs and cook it until it sets a bit
  4. Immediately add rice and toss everything around
  5. Add soy sauce for more flavor and color
  6. Add MSG for more umami
  7. Add white pepper
  8. Lastly, Add green onions
  9. Plate and enjoy!
