Scrambled Eggs

Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

This Scrambled Eggs recipes is guaranteed to elevate your breakfast

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Beat 2-3 eggs until no egg whites are visible
  2. Add salt to the beaten eggs
  3. Add cold butter to a hot pan and wait for it to foam up
  4. Add the eggs and slowly move the eggs around avoiding and brown bits
  5. Cook the eggs just before it fully sets
  6. Add Pepper
  7. Plate and garnish with Chives
